Downstream

Leveraging over two decades of industry experience, Algrate's downstream expertise includes sourcing products, bulk sales, and distributing petroleum products worldwide. The company operates storage terminals throughout Africa, the Middle East, and Europe, boasting a total capacity exceeding 300 million liters for various refined petroleum products. Recently, the Group has made significant strides in Eastern Africa to bolster the distribution of refined products in the region.

UPstream

Algrate's Upstream division stands out as one of Africa's premier independent exploration and production companies, boasting a varied portfolio of eight oil and gas assets in rich basins throughout the continent. Leading our upstream efforts are Asharami Energy Limited and Sahara Energy Field Holdings UK Limited. These assets span different development phases, from exploratory sites to mature production fields, presenting significant opportunities for favorable returns. Asharami Energy is committed to acquiring, developing, and optimizing oil and gas assets in both established and emerging markets across Africa.
